About the shop

The art and design shop will be both educational and recreational – home to the emerging talent of our students, but also an innovative gateway for the public, connecting the University with the city and Belfast’s vibrant artistic community.

Find out more

Sell Your Work

This is your chance to gain exposure, make professional connections and sell your work. We are looking for pieces of art work from Belfast School of Art students, across all years and disciplines, as well as from our alumni.

Volunteer

This project is run by students for students. Anyone can volunteer for the duration of the show and it is the perfect way to boost your employability skills as all volunteer hours will count towards the EDGE Award.
